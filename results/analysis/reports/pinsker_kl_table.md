## Reading guide

- **KL_LB ≈ 0**: the audit cannot distinguish forget from retain at this scale. The method may still be adversarially fragile, but any selective-leakage claim is empirically vacuous (Corollary 2.1).
- **KL_LB > 0**: the binary recoverability outcome carries at least this much variational information about forget-set membership. Selective leakage at scale `eps_max` is empirically certified at the rate shown.
- The Pinsker bound is loose near `p_F, p_R` close to 0 or 1; the Bretagnolle-Huber column is tighter there.
